About the Game

Traveling to another country can be scary, especially one that speaks a different language from you. Far too many people try to travel abroad with no knowledge of the culture of the country they're visiting. We created "Nihon Quest" as a fun and engaging way to teach players about the culture and language of Japan. This game seeks to improve the player's cultural awareness and promote intercultural competence.
